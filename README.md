# Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Dashboard

Domain: Retail Analytics, Business Intelligence

## Problem Statement

Blinkit, one of India’s leading instant delivery platforms, manages a large and complex dataset covering **sales, items, and outlet operations** across multiple regions. Despite having rich data, the lack of a **single consolidated analytical view** made it difficult for leadership to track performance, understand customer preferences, and evaluate outlet efficiency.

The challenge was to convert fragmented, multi-year data into a **business-ready Power BI dashboard** that enables quick, data-driven decisions with minimal friction.


## Project Objective

The objective of this project was to design a **scalable and interactive Power BI dashboard** that consolidates Blinkit’s sales, item, and outlet data to:

* Identify **revenue and sales trends** across regions, outlet types, and product categories
* Analyze **item performance, customer preferences, and fat content distribution** for healthier product insights
* Evaluate **outlet performance and growth patterns** to support strategic expansion
* Enable business users to **filter and drill down** into metrics seamlessly

## Approach & Execution

### Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Standardized and renamed columns for clarity and consistency
* Removed duplicates and handled missing values across datasets (2012–2022)
* Ensured consistent item categorization and outlet attributes for accurate comparisons

### Data Modeling & Integration

* Built relationships between **sales, items, and outlet** tables
* Created calculated measures using DAX for:

  * Total Sales
  * Average Sales
  * Number of Items
  * Average Rating

This enabled dynamic filtering and cross-table analysis.

### Exploratory Analysis & Key Insights

**Sales Performance**

* Total sales reached **$1.20M** with an average transaction value of **$141**
* Clear revenue concentration across specific outlet types and regions

**Item Analysis**

* Items categorized into core segments such as **Fruits & Vegetables, Snack Foods, and Household items**
* Fat content distribution analyzed to support **health-focused product strategy**

**Outlet Performance**

* Medium-sized outlets generated **$507.9K** in sales
* Tier 3 locations contributed **$472.13K**, indicating strong growth potential

**Outlet Categories**

* **Supermarket Type 1** emerged as the top performer:

  * Sales: **$787.55K**
  * Item Count: **5,577**
* Other outlet types contributed complementary revenue streams


### Dashboard Design & Interactivity

* Designed intuitive **slicers** for:

  * Outlet location
  * Outlet size
  * Item type
* Displayed detailed **KPIs by outlet type**, including:

  * Total sales
  * Number of items
  * Average transaction value
  * Average rating
  * Visibility metrics
* Used clean, business-friendly visualizations to highlight:

  * Trends
  * Correlations
  * Growth patterns

## Key Metrics Tracked

* Total Sales
* Average Sales Value
* Item Count
* Average Rating
* Sales by Outlet Type
* Sales by Tier and Outlet Size
* Item Category & Fat Content Distribution

## Outcome & Business Impact

The dashboard transformed Blinkit’s fragmented datasets into a **single source of truth** for business performance. It empowered leadership to:

* Identify **revenue growth opportunities** by outlet type and geography
* Optimize the **product portfolio** based on item performance and customer preferences
* Allocate resources strategically toward **medium-sized outlets and high-performing Tier 3 locations**

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how effective data modeling and visualization can convert raw retail data into **clear, actionable insights**, supporting smarter decisions in fast-paced, operations-heavy businesses like Blinkit.
